Recipe

  1. Heat the oven to 190C/170C fan/gas 5. Cream the butter in a large bowl with a wooden spoon
    or in a stand mixer until it is soft. Add the sugar and keep beating until the mixture is light and fluffy.
  2. Roll the dough into walnut-sized balls and place them slightly apart from each other on a baking sheet
  3. atten the balls a little with the palm of your hand and bake them in the oven for around 10-12 mins until they are golden brown and slightly firm on top
  4. Leave the cookies on a cooling rack for around 15 mins before serving.
